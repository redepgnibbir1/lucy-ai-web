## Content Strategy Notes

### Priority Ranking
1. **High Priority (Publish First):** Articles 1, 2, 5, 6, 7 – Target highest volume keywords and strongest pain points
2. **Medium Priority:** Articles 3, 4, 8, 9, 10, 11 – Good keyword targeting with clear feature tie-ins
3. **Supporting Content:** Articles 12-20 – Build topical authority and capture long-tail traffic

### SEO Considerations
- Target both English and Swedish markets (consider localized versions)
- Target featured snippets with clear H2/H3 structure and answer-focused content
- Include internal links between related articles to build topic clusters
- Add schema markup for FAQ sections where applicable

### Conversion Optimization
- Every article should have a clear, relevant CTA
- Include social proof (customer quotes, statistics) where possible
- Offer downloadable resources (checklists, templates) for lead capture
- Add demo booking widgets inline for high-intent articles

### Content Format Recommendations
- **Pillar Pages (2,500+ words):** Articles 1, 2, 7, 14
- **Standard Blog Posts (1,200-1,800 words):** Articles 3-6, 8-13, 15-18
- **Comparison/Listicles (1,000-1,500 words):** Articles 4, 19, 20
